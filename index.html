About My Stories

My stories blend supernatural thrills with deeper spiritual themes, exploring the hidden dimensions of our world through a lens of faith and scientific discovery. They're supernatural thrillers that examine spiritual warfare, divine truth, and the mysteries of creation in unexpected ways.

Perfect for readers who enjoy the intersection of science and faith, and stories that challenge conventional thinking about spirituality.

About The Author

Lily Rosslyn

Inspired by the legendary works of J.R.R. Tolkien and C.S. Lewis, Lily Rosslyn weaves enchanting stories that unveil the epic battles between light and shadow in unseen realms. By blending Urban Supernatural, Science Fiction, and Fantasy, she explores the fine line between human ambition and the supernatural powers shaping our universe, all while showcasing our divine strength to conquer darkness.
